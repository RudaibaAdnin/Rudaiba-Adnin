Please visit my Research Projects Page to see the detailed list of my completed and ongoing works.

My research focus is situated at the intersection of computer science and social sciences. My interests are Social Computing, Empirical Studies, Data Analysis, Human-computer Interaction, Assistive Technology, and Machine Learning.

I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and technology (BUET) in 2021. I worked on social computing with Dr. A.B.M Alim Al Islam, Dr. Sriram Chelappan, Dr. Sadia Sharmin, and Dr. Anindya Iqbal.
